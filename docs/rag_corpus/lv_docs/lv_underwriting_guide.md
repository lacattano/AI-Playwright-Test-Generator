## 1. Scope

This guide defines the underwriting rules, rating factors, and decline criteria for LV Motor Insurance products. All quotes must comply with the rules herein. Deviations require underwriter approval.

---

## 2. Products

### 2.1 Car Insurance

Three schemes are available:

| Scheme | Description | Premium Adjustment | Key Features |
|--------|-------------|-------------------|--------------|
| **Standard** | Base level cover | Baseline | Roadside assistance included |
| **Premier** | Enhanced cover | +£100/year | European cover, courtesy car, motor legal |
| **Classic** | Vehicles over 20 years old | −£50/year | Specialist cover, agreed value |

### 2.2 Home Insurance

Home Insurance is offered as a separate product. This guide covers Motor only. Refer to UW-HOME-2026 for home underwriting rules.

## 3. Rating Factors

### 3.1 Base Premium

The annual premium is calculated from a base rate of **£450**, adjusted by the factors below.

### 3.2 Vehicle Factors

#### 3.2.1 Vehicle Value

A loading of **3% of the vehicle's estimated market value** is added to the base premium.

*Example:* A vehicle valued at £15,000 adds £450 to the premium (3% × £15,000).

#### 3.2.2 Engine Size

| Engine Capacity | Surcharge |
|-----------------|-----------|
| Under 1600cc | £0 |
| 1600cc – 1999cc | +£60 |
| 2000cc and above | +£120 |

#### 3.2.3 Vehicle Age

| Vehicle Age | Adjustment | Rationale |
|-------------|-----------|-----------|
| 0–2 years | +£80 | Higher repair costs for new vehicles |
| 3–9 years | £0 | Standard risk |
| 10+ years | −£50 | Lower market value reduces exposure |

### 3.3 Driver Factors

#### 3.3.1 Age of Main Driver

| Age Band | Adjustment |
|----------|-----------|
| Under 25 | +£200 |
| 25–29 | +£100 |
| 30–49 | £0 (baseline) |
| 50 and over | −£50 |

Young drivers (under 25) with penalty points of 6 or more are subject to an additional compulsory excess of £200 (see Section 6.1).

#### 3.3.2 Driving Experience

| Years Licensed | Adjustment |
|----------------|-----------|
| Under 3 years | +£150 |
| 3–4 years | +£80 |
| 5+ years | £0 |

#### 3.3.3 Penalty Points

| Points on License | Surcharge | Excess Impact |
|-------------------|-----------|---------------|
| 0 | £0 | None |
| 3 | +£50 | None |
| 6 | +£120 | +£150 compulsory excess |
| 9 | +£180 | +£150 compulsory excess |
| 12 | **DECLINE** | N/A |

**Rule:** Any driver with 12 or more penalty points must be declined. Points are counted across all drivers named on the policy.

#### 3.3.4 Accident History (Last 5 Years)

| Accidents | Loading |
|-----------|---------|
| 0 | £0 |
| 1 | +£100 |
| 2 or more | +£200 |

Accident loading applies to the main driver only. Additional driver accidents are loaded at 60% of the main driver rate.

#### 3.3.5 Claims History (Last 5 Years)

| Claims | Loading |
|--------|---------|
| 0 | £0 |
| 1 | +£80 |
| 2 or more | +£150 |

#### 3.3.6 Occupation Risk Categories

Occupations are classified as Standard or High Risk:

**High Risk Occupations** (+£60 loading): Builder, Driver, Electrician, Mechanic, Plumber, Journalist.

All other occupations are Standard Risk (no loading).

#### 3.3.7 Additional Drivers

Each additional driver is rated individually using the same factors as the main driver (age, experience, points, accidents). The loading is added to the policy premium.

### 3.4 No Claims Discount (NCD)

NCD reduces the premium by **5% per claim-free year**, up to a maximum of **40%** (8 years).

NCD is applied after all other loadings are calculated.

The NCD holder must be named as a driver on the policy.

### 3.5 Overnight Location

| Location | Adjustment |
|----------|-----------|
| Private Garage | −£30 |
| Private Driveway | £0 |
| Street Outside Home | +£40 |
| Public Car Park | +£70 |

### 3.6 Geographic (Postcode) Rating

Postcodes are rated by area prefix. The following prefixes attract a **+£80** loading:

B, M, L, N, E, NW, SE (Birmingham, Manchester, Liverpool, North London, East London, North West London, South East London).

All other postcodes are standard rate.

## 4. Usage Types

### 4.1 Social, Domestic & Pleasure (SDP)

Standard cover. No restrictions beyond mileage.

### 4.2 Business Use

Covers commuting to a single place of work and incidental business travel. No premium loading but requires the policyholder's occupation to match.

### 4.3 Commercial Use

**DECLINE RULE:** Commercial use (carriage of goods for hire and reward, delivery services, taxi/private hire) is **not covered** under any LV Motor product. Quotes with commercial use selected must be declined immediately. Do not refer — there is no appeal.

## 6. Excesses

### 6.1 Compulsory Excess

The base compulsory excess is **£250**.

Additional compulsory excess applies in the following situations (cumulative):

| Condition | Additional Excess |
|-----------|-------------------|
| Main driver under 25 | +£200 |
| Main driver with 6+ penalty points | +£150 |
| Vehicle engine 2000cc or above | +£100 |
| Vehicle less than 3 years old | +£50 |

### 6.2 Voluntary Excess

Customers may select a voluntary excess of **£0–£500** in increments of £50.

Each £50 of voluntary excess reduces the annual premium by approximately **£15**.

## 7. Optional Extras

### 7.1 No Claims Discount Guarantee

**Cost:** £45/year

Protects the customer's NCD in the event of a single claim. The NCD level is preserved even if a claim is made.

### 7.2 Breakdown Cover

| Level | Cost | Coverage |
|-------|------|----------|
| Roadside | £35/year | Roadside assistance within ¼ mile of home |
| Full UK Recovery | £75/year | Roadside + recovery to any UK destination |

### 7.3 Courtesy Car

| Level | Cost | Vehicle Provided |
|-------|------|------------------|
| Small Car | £50/year | Compact vehicle (e.g., Corsa, Fiesta) |
| Similar Size | £90/year | Vehicle of comparable size to insured vehicle |

### 7.4 Motor Legal Protection

**Cost:** £28/year

Up to £100,000 legal expenses cover for pursuing claims against third parties.

## 8. Payment Options

### 8.1 Pay in Full

Single card payment for the full annual premium. No additional charges.

### 8.2 Deposit + Monthly Instalments

20% deposit by card today, remainder spread over **10 monthly payments** at **19.9% APR representative**.

The monthly instalment is calculated as: `(remaining_balance × 1.199) ÷ 10`.

### 8.3 Deposit + Bank Transfer

20% deposit by card today, remainder by bank transfer within 14 days. No interest charged. Policy cover begins upon receipt of the full amount.

---

## 9. Decline Rules Summary

The following **must** result in an immediate decline:

1. **Commercial use** selected (Section 4.3)
2. **12 or more penalty points** on any named driver (Section 3.3.3)
3. **Vehicle valued over £100,000** (requires specialist underwriter — do not quote)

The decline message must state the specific reason. No quote reference is generated for declined applications.

## 10. Document Delivery Preferences

Customers may select from:

- **Email only** — all documents sent as PDF attachments
- **Post only** — paper documents sent to the address on file
- **Both** — email PDFs plus paper copies

Accessible format options:
- **Braille** — documents produced in Grade 2 Braille
- **Large print** — documents in 16pt font

Accessible format requests add 3–5 working days to document delivery time.
